When using AI through kenh-ai, whether via the chat interface or the API, your prompts and responses go through multiple touchpoints. You control how your data is handled at each step.

This page gives an overview of how your data is stored and used by kenh-ai. More information is available in the [privacy policy](https://kenh-ai.com/privacy) and [terms of service](https://kenh-ai.com/terms).

## Within kenh-ai

kenh-ai does not store your prompts or responses, _unless_ you opt in to one or both of the following:

- **Private Input & Output Logging:** Make your prompts and completions visible in your logs for debugging, comparing model responses, and optimizing prompts. kenh-ai does not access or use this data. For organizations, only admins can view logged data. Off by default. Enable it in your [Observability settings](https://kenh-ai.com/workspaces/default/observability).
- **kenh-ai Use of Inputs/Outputs:** Allow kenh-ai to use your prompt and completion data to improve the product. In exchange, you receive a 1% discount on all model usage. Off by default. Enable it in your [Privacy settings](https://kenh-ai.com/workspaces/default/settings).

_Anonymous Input Categorization: kenh-ai samples a small number of prompts for categorization to power our reporting and model ranking. If you are not opted in to kenh-ai use of inputs/outputs, any categorization of your prompts is stored completely anonymously and never associated with your account or user ID. The categorization is done by model with a zero-data-retention policy._

## Metadata Collection

kenh-ai does store metadata (e.g. number of prompt and completion tokens, latency, etc) for each request. This is used to power our reporting and model ranking, and your [logs metadata](https://kenh-ai.com/logs).

This metadata does not include the content of your prompts or responses, only information about the request itself.
